SideLinker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

1. Data Collection & Usage

SideLinker is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) to external servers.

All device connection data, Mac server addresses, and control commands are processed and stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions Disclosure

To function properly, SideLinker requires the following permissions:

  • Local Network: Used to communicate with the SideLinker Mac server component to trigger Sidecar and system commands.
  • Bluetooth: Used for proximity detection to enable "Near-field Awakening" features.
  • iCloud: Used exclusively for syncing your Pro status and connection preferences across your own devices via Apple’s secure infrastructure.

3. Third-party Services

We use Apple StoreKit for In-App Purchases. Any financial data is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or the SideLinker app, please contact us at: support@sidelinker.app.
