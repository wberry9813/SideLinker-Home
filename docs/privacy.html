SideLinker Privacy Policy Last Updated: 2026-03-05 1. Data Collection & Usage SideLinker is designed with privacy as a core principle. No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) to external servers. Local Processing: All device connection data, Mac server addresses, and control commands are processed and stored locally on your device. 2. Permissions Disclosure Bluetooth: Used for proximity detection to enable "Near-field Awakening" features. Local Network: Used to communicate with the SideLinker Mac server component. iCloud: Used exclusively for syncing your Pro status and connection preferences across your own devices via Apple’s secure infrastructure. 3. Third-party Services We use Apple StoreKit for In-App Purchases. Any financial data is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. 4.
